How Information is Collected

Nonpublic personal information

Nonpublic personal information may include, but is not limited to, your name, phone number, postal address, and email address ("Personal Information"). You can use our website without disclosing Personal Information. If, however, you wish to access certain products or services that we make available through our website, you may be required to provide Personal Information. We only collect Personal Information that you voluntarily provide. We collect this information while you are visiting this website, to identify and communicate with you, to respond to your requests, to process your transaction or to provide services requested or authorized by you, and to improve our services.

Browser Log Data

Like all websites, we automatically collect information that your browser sends whenever you visit our website ("Browser Log Data"). This information does not identify you to us unless you have given us your name, contact information, or other Personal Information. This Browser Log Data may include information such as your computer's Internet Protocol ("IP") address, browser type, browser version, operating system, domain names, and browsing history. It also includes a record of the pages of our website that you visit, time and date of your visit, time spent on those pages, and other statistics.

Web Cookies

We may send a "cookie" to your computer. A cookie is a small piece of data that is sent to your Internet browser from a web server and is stored on your computer's hard drive. Cookies are used as a reliable mechanism for remembering user activity on a website, allowing for improved services. We use cookies to identify and keep track of information to better serve you when visiting our website, to save you time when you are here, and to provide you with a more meaningful and productive website experience. The cookies we use do not collect Personal Information, and we do not use them to determine your identity. If you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some features of our website.

Third Party Services

We may use third-party services, like Google Analytics, to collect, monitor, and analyze visitor data (such as Browser Log Data and cookies) in order to increase our website's functionality. If these third party services collect information, they do so anonymously and in the aggregate, to provide information that is helpful to us, such as website trends. They do not identify individual visitors. These third-party service providers have their own privacy policies addressing how they use such information. If you want to prevent your data from being used by Google Analytics, you may wish to take advantage of Google Analytics' currently available opt-outs for the web.
